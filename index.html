About Me

I've been working in digital marketing and product management for around fifteen years mostly for business to consumer companies in the food and energy sectors. I've always worked best when I sit between the client and the development team as a kind of 'translator' or requirements back and forth.
I know that many business owners find their requirements difficult to shape and even more difficult to find a good development team to work with, so earlier this year I was inspired to start The Good Webwhere I could provide a simple and easy service to my clients and create beautiful websites suited to the unique needs of individual clients. So whether you're looking for a 'basic' site that you update once every blue moon or you want to upload a blog and social media feed every hour of the day I have the understanding to match the site to your needs.
